Last Updated: February 6, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Prime Bot only collects the minimum data required for its features to function. This includes:

  • User IDs and Guild IDs for leveling and moderation.
  • Message content (only when processed for AI chat or moderation triggers).
  • Role information for portfolio generation.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Calculating ranks and building your web portfolios.
  • Providing AI-driven responses and moderation.
  • Finding and delivering requested assets.

3. Data Retentions

We store data as long as the bot remains in your server or as long as you maintain a rank. Portfolios are generated dynamically based on your current server status.

4. Third-Party Services

We use external AI providers to process chat messages. These providers do not receive any identifying information like your email or real name, only the message content you send to the bot.

5. Your Rights

You can request data deletion at any time by contacting our support through Discord or by leaving the server where the bot is present (though some metadata may persist for server continuity).

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. However, no internet-based service is 100% secure.
